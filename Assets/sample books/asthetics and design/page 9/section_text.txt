Aesthetics Is Compression

You may think aesthetics adds decoration. At a deeper level, it removes noise. Good aesthetics compresses many signals into a form that is easy to process without losing meaning.

Look at a well-designed symbol. It uses minimal lines, yet you recognize it instantly. The form carries high information with low complexity. In contrast, a cluttered design forces you to process many unnecessary details to get the same meaning.

The highest level of aesthetics is efficient encoding. Maximum clarity with minimum elements. When a form feels "clean" or "elegant," it is because your brain extracts meaning quickly with little effort.
