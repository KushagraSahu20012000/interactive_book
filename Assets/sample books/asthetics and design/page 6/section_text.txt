Aesthetic Judgment Is a System

You may treat taste as a feeling that appears instantly. At a higher level, it is a system you can run deliberately. Instead of "I like this," you can evaluate using clear variables: structure, contrast, coherence, and context-fit.

Take a mobile app screen. You can check: Is the layout structured or scattered? Is contrast used to guide attention or is everything competing? Do elements follow a consistent logic? Does the design match its use, like speed for a payment app or calm for a meditation app? Each question reduces guesswork.

When you turn taste into a system, your decisions become repeatable. You can explain why something works, improve it step by step, and transfer the skill across domains - design, writing, music, or environments.
