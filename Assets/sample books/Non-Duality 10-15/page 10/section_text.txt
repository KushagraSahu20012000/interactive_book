Nothing Was Ever Separate

You searched in thoughts, body, emotions.
You watched them come and go.
You even saw the sense of "I" appear and disappear.
What remains is not inside or outside—it simply is.

Look now.
There is seeing, hearing, feeling.
No gap between you and what is seen.
No boundary where "you" end and the world begins.
Only one continuous happening.

There are not two—observer and observed.
Only knowing, appearing as everything.
This is why nothing was ever separate.
"My-self" is not a person. It is all that is.
