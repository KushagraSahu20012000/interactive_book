Nothing Needs to Be Removed

You may think: "I must stop thoughts to be clear."
Or: "I must become calm first."
This assumes something is blocking you.

Watch carefully.
A loud thought appears.
At the same time, you are aware of it.
The awareness is already clear—even while the thought is noisy.

Try this: sit and let everything happen.
Sounds, thoughts, emotions—no control.
Notice: nothing touches the one that knows them.
Clarity is not something you create. It is already there.
