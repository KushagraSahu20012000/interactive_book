No Effort Is Required

You may think: "I need to stay aware."
That becomes another task.
Another pressure.

But notice—are you not already aware right now?
Even the thought "I am distracted" is being known.
You did not create this knowing. It is already present.

Try to stop being aware.
Not control thoughts—just stop knowing anything.
You cannot.
Because awareness is not something you do. It is what you are.
