Attention Chooses What Becomes "Real"

Many things are happening right now.
Sounds, sensations, thoughts.
But you only notice a few.
The rest fade into the background.

Sit quietly and listen.
At first, you hear your own thoughts.
Then a fan.
Then a distant sound outside.
Each time attention moves, a new part of the world becomes clear.

What you call "your experience" is just what attention is touching.
But the one who notices attention moving does not move.
Attention shifts. The knower stays.
