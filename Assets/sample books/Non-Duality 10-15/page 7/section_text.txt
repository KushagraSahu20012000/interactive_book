The Sense of "I" Is Also Seen

A thought says, "I am doing this."
It feels like the center, the owner.
But look closely—can you notice this "I" as it appears?

While solving a problem, a voice says, "I understand."
When you fail, it says, "I am not good enough."
This "I" keeps changing—confident, doubtful, proud, ashamed.

If it changes, it cannot be the real center.
It is just another thought being noticed.
Even the feeling of "me" is something you can observe.
