So What Is Left?

Your thoughts change.
Your body changes.
Your emotions change.
So what is left?

Look at your life from a distance.
Five years ago—you had different fears, different ideas, a smaller body.
Today—all of that is different.
But something has been present in every moment, silently aware.

That presence does not grow or shrink.
It does not get louder or quieter.
It simply knows whatever appears—thought, body, or feeling.
That constant knower is what you are.
