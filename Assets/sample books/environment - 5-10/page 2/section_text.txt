Care Leaves Marks

A girl found a clear glass bottle by the sea,
She said, “No one will notice me.”
She threw it far into the blue,
It sank where tiny fishes grew.
Days went by, she came once more,
And saw the same glass on the shore.

A crab was stuck, its claw held tight,
A bird nearby could barely fly right.
She looked around, then looked inside,
“No one saw me…” she softly cried.
But marks were there, they stayed behind,
On water, air, and living kind.

What you do does not just fade,
The world remembers every trade.
